Last updated: November 2025

How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain the MindCoach AI service.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted providers who help us operate the service (for example, Stripe for payments and hosting providers for running the app), subject to appropriate security and privacy protections.

Data security
